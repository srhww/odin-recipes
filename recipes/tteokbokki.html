Tteokbokki

Tteokbokki is made of rice cakes and a spicy sauce using gochujang.

Ingredients

Main

  • 350g Korean rice cakes
  • 150g Korean fish cakes
  • 2 cups Korean soup stock
  • 60g thinly sliced onions

Tteokbokki sauce (mix these in a bowl)

  • 3 Tbsp gochujang
  • 1 1/2 Tbsp raw sugar
  • 1 Tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp minced garlic
  • 1 tsp gochugaru

Garnish

  • 1 tsp roasted sesame seeds
  • 1 tsp seame oil
  • 1 stalk green onion, finely chopped

Steps

  1. Soak your rice cakes in warm water for 10 minutes.
  2. Boil soup stock in a shallow pot and dissolve tteokbokki sauce. Once boiled, add rice cakes, fish cakes and onion. Boil for an additional 3 to 5 minutes until rice cakes are fully cooked. Then simmer for a further 4 minutes.
  3. Add the sesame oil, sesame seeds, and green onions, then quickly stir.
  4. Serve warm!
